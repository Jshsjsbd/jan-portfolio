About Me

I’m Jan Peter, a multifaceted creator driven by curiosity, passion, and the pursuit of excellence. With a unique blend of technical expertise and artistic vision, I craft digital experiences that are not only functional but also immersive and visually stunning. As a developer, I specialize in modern web technologies, leveraging frameworks like Three.js, GSAP, and React to bring ideas to life. From building sleek user interfaces to creating complex 3D animations, I take pride in solving real-world problems with creative solutions.

Beyond coding, I’m an avid pianist and drummer, channeling my emotions into melodies that resonate with those around me. I’m also a competitive swimmer, having participated in national-level championships, which has taught me discipline, perseverance, and the value of teamwork. Passionate about cybersecurity, my ultimate goal is to become a FullStack Developer and cybersecurity expert, combining my love for problem-solving with my fascination for technology. At the same time, I strive to grow spiritually, seeking a deeper connection with faith and purpose in everything I do.
